Discoverers Program for Toddlers under 2

TOI Most Valued Mother & Child Brands 2020, Kido Schools, launches “Discoverers Program”, an engaging parent-toddler program for children under 2.

The program will be thrice a week ( Mon | Wed | Fri or Tue | Thur | Sat ) and will be conducted by our experienced Early Years educators.

Our accompanying program will be limited to only 6 toddlers per batch and will be for a duration of 45 minutes, that will include:

  • Yoga
  • Skills Motor
  • Toddler kits
  • Sensory activities
  • Skill-building activities
  • Learning modules Interactive
  • Interactive learning modules
  • Exploring & nurturing the brain
  • Social & life skill development
  • Bonding time between parents & child

Our first batch of Discoverers Program starts on 16 August. We invite parents to register themselves with us at the earliest.
